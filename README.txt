SHIFTLIFE SWAST — GLOUCESTERSHIRE
====================================

TO INSTALL ON YOUR PHONE
==========================
1. Go to app.netlify.com/drop on a computer
2. Drag this entire folder onto the page
3. You get a free URL instantly
4. Open that URL on your phone in Chrome
5. Tap the three-dot menu → Add to Home Screen
6. Opens like a normal app from your home screen

HOW TO USE
===========
- Tap any day on the calendar to add your shift
- Set shift type, station, start and finish times
- Use the − / + buttons to log any overrun (5 min steps)
- Log your break times and tick if taken off-station (£7.50 allowance)
- Check the End of Shift tab to see your protection schedule
- Set your pay band in Settings for accurate pay estimates
- Scroll down to see your monthly stats

Your data stays on your phone — nothing is sent anywhere.
