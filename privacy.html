Privacy Policy

Who we are

KineticShift is an independent mobile web application developed by a frontline NHS ambulance worker to help shift workers track their working hours, pay, and relief hours. The app is operated as a sole trader business.

What data we collect

When you use KineticShift, we collect and store the following information:

  • Google account information — your email address and a unique user ID, collected when you sign in with Google. We do not store your password.
  • Shift data — shift types, start and end times, locations, overtime hours, overrun times, notes, and mileage that you enter into the app.
  • Pay settings — your AfC band, hourly rate, and break deduction preferences.
  • Profile information — your name, station, trust name, and NHS start date if you choose to enter these.
  • Relief tracker data — GRS hours and actual hours worked per month.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your shifts and pay calculations within the app
  • Sync your data across your devices when you are signed in
  • Calculate your estimated pay and relief balance

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — in your browser's local storage, so the app works even without an internet connection.
  • Google Firebase Firestore — a secure cloud database provided by Google, when you choose to sign in. Your data is stored in Google's data centres in Europe (London region). Google's privacy policy applies to this storage: policies.google.com/privacy

Data security

Your cloud data is protected by Firebase security rules that ensure only you — when signed in with your Google account — can read or write your own data. No other user or third party can access your shift data.

Your rights under UK GDPR

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • Access your data — you can view all your data directly within the app
  • Delete your data — use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings, which removes data from your device. To request deletion of your cloud data, contact us below.
  • Export your data — use the "Export Backup" option in Settings to download all your data as a JSON file.
  • Correct your data — you can edit any information directly within the app.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. If you delete your account or request data deletion, your cloud data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Children's privacy

KineticShift is intended for adult NHS and emergency service workers only. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated date.
