Breaks (SWAST): Under 6hrs = no break · 6hrs to 11h59m = 30 min unpaid · 12hrs = 1 hour unpaid. If break not taken, go home early — pay stays the same.
Sick pay: Basic rate only (7.5hrs × hourly rate), no unsocial. If sent home more than halfway through shift — NOT a sick day, full pay applies including unsocial.
Mileage: Different station = claim shorter of home→new or base→new at 45p/mile.
Unsocial hours: Before 08:00 or after 20:00 Mon–Fri, or any time Sat/Sun. Enhancement applied on top of base rate.
Overrun: ×1.5 rate from minute 1. Log in 5 min increments per SWAST policy.
EoS protection: 60 min — home area Cat 1/2 · 30 min — Cat 1 & P1 only · 5 min — confirmed cardiac arrest with CPR only.
Annual leave: SWAST pays basic rate only. Employment law may entitle you to average pay including unsocial — speak to your union rep.
