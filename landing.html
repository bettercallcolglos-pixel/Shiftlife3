Features
Everything you need to
understand your pay
No more guessing. No more checking payslips three times. KineticShift does the maths so you don't have to.
📅
Shift Calendar
Log Days, Nights, Mids, Late shifts, Annual Leave, Sick days and Overtime. Tap any day to add or edit a shift in seconds.
💰
AfC Pay Calculator
Automatically calculates basic pay, unsocial hours enhancement (30%), Sunday enhancement (60%), and overtime at x1.5.
🔄
Rolling Relief Tracker
Track your GRS hours against actual hours worked. Includes overrun time. Shows your running balance so you always know where you stand.
📊
Year Overview
See your estimated gross pay month by month and year to date. Understand your earnings at a glance.
☁️
Cloud Sync
Sign in with Google and your shifts sync automatically across all your devices. Phone, tablet, computer — always up to date.
📤
Export Your Data
Export your shifts to CSV for Excel, or back up everything as a JSON file. Your data is always yours to keep.
How it works
Up and running in minutes
1
Open the app
No download needed. Open kineticshift.online in your phone browser and tap "Add to Home Screen."
2
Set your pay rate
Enter your AfC band and hourly rate in Settings. Takes 30 seconds.
3
Log your shifts
Tap any day on the calendar and add your shift. The app calculates everything instantly.
4
Know your pay
See your estimated gross pay for the month — broken down into basic, unsocial, Sunday, and OT.
